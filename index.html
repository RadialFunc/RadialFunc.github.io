Achievements in Math Competitions

I qualified for the 2021 AIME through both the AMC 10A and B. I have made top 10 in Mathleague multiple times, got a 23 on the AMC 8, and am doing well on other competitions such as 9th place on LMT and more. In addition, I placed 6th in Mathleague Nationals for the 6th grade division.

Discord Bots

On the coding side, I have made many discord bots. For example, I made a Math Countdown bot that uses past AIME problems to create a countdown match with customizable time limits and problem numbers. You can find more info here. I also coded TheCALT PoTD Bot, a bot that sends out a daily problem at 12:00 AM UTC for people to solve. That can be found in TheCALT Discord Server.
